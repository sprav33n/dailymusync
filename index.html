06 September 2019

A while ago when I had to cleanup all the lamps, I was justifying in my mind that even an electric lamp would be as good as the traditional fire lamps and that the god wouldn't differentiate between the two. Today when I finally cleaned, dried and lit the lamp I realized that the electric one will never the same effect of the flame glistening, reflecting and bouncing off not only the inner walls of the lamp but also the golden hues of the oil!
